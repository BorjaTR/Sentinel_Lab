What is Sentinel Score?

Sentinel Score is a 0-100 rating that measures protocol financial sustainability. It combines four components:

  • Runway (40%) - Months until treasury depletion
  • Sustainability (30%) - Revenue vs costs ratio
  • Diversification (20%) - Treasury and revenue concentration risk
  • Trajectory (10%) - Historical trend and improvements
