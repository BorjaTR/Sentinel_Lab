What is Sentinel Score?

Sentinel Score is a 0-100 rating that measures protocol financial sustainability. It combines four components:

  • Runway (40%) - Months until treasury depletion at current burn rate
  • Sustainability (30%) - Revenue vs costs ratio (break-even is 1.0)
  • Diversification (20%) - Treasury and revenue concentration risk using HHI
  • Trajectory (10%) - Historical trend and financial improvements
